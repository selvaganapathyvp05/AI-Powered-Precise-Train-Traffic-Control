TRAIN INFORMATION

A train is a railway vehicle used to transport passengers and goods between different locations. It runs on railway tracks and is powered by diesel, electricity, or other energy sources.

Main Components:
- Locomotive (Engine)
- Passenger Coaches
- Freight Wagons
- Tracks
- Signals
- Platforms

Types of Trains:
- Passenger Train
- Express Train
- Superfast Train
- Metro Train
- High-Speed Train

Railway Operations:
- Train Scheduling
- Platform Allocation
- Track Management
- Signal Control
- Delay Monitoring
- Traffic Control

Common Railway Challenges:
- Train Delays
- Platform Conflicts
- Track Congestion
- Signal Failures
- Weather Disruptions

Role of AI:
Artificial Intelligence can analyze train schedules, predict delays, detect conflicts, optimize platform allocation, and recommend better routing decisions to improve railway safety and efficiency.

Project:
AI-Powered Precise Train Traffic Control
This project aims to build an intelligent railway traffic management system that predicts delays, detects scheduling conflicts, and assists railway operators in making faster and more accurate traffic control decisions.
